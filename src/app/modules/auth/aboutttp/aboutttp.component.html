Features and Purpose Of
Education Management
check_circle_outlineEnquiries and Admission Management
With the help of the Education Management, the most commonly asked questions and enquiries regarding admissions, rules and policies can be easily answered. This can free up a lot of time and resources which can be allocated elsewhere.

Transition of school management from traditional manual to digital can be made easy by using Bulk excel data upload, Admission Management.

check_circle_outlineManagement of Fee Collections
Fee collection can be made easy with the help of the Educational Management, as it calculates and displays fee amount, due and payment details.
check_circle_outlineExamination Management
An Education Management can be useful for planning, scheduling, and informing important information about exams by using scheduling. It can also be used to publish exam results that can be accessed by the students and teachers. Further detailed reports can be created using this data, and based on this, student performance can be tracked.
check_circle_outlineStudent Information Management
This feature helps teachers and parents to access essential information on students' academic activities quickly and easily. This may include fee attendance records, exam results, payment history, and general academic progress.
check_circle_outlineAttendance Management
Attendance tracking and its management is made entirely easier. Teachers and school administrators do not need to track attendance manually but simply evaluate automatically generated reports and check where there are issues.
check_circle_outlineLesson Plans & Assignments
An Education Management makes it easy for teachers to share assignments and lesson plans with students and their parents in advance. These can be seen online by using Evaluation.
check_circle_outlineAutomatic Certificate Generation
Using Education Management, Students' education related certificate and other sports activities can be generated and print easily.
check_circle_outlineTimetable management
This function allows you to quickly organize and sort out the subjects and time allocated to each of them according to the class and teacher. A hurriedly made timetable will only bring confusion and more hurdles in running your educational institute smoothly.
check_circle_outlineEvaluation and Assessment
This module enables to conduct online test and exams. Evaluation or assessment of the students can be conducted by defining question and answer that can be re-used over and over again that can saves teachers' huge amount of time.
